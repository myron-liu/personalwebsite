About Me

My name is Myron Liu and I am a recent graduate from the University of California, Berkeley majoring in Computer Science, Statistics, and Chemical Engineering. Last summer, I worked as a software engineering intern at Arista Networks. I am currently looking for Full Stack software engineering roles in the Bay Area.

I am passionate about technology and the ways it can open up freedoms in people's lives and improve our ability to explore the world. My primary goal is to build things that matter and have a positive impact on the people around me.

My technical background includes experience with machine learning, deep reinforcement learning, data visualization, system and database design and computer security. My primary coding languages are Go, Python, and Javascript. I am proficient at Java and C. I have worked with MySQL, Postgres, Hbase, and MongoDB databases. I am comfortable with React, React Native, Node, Express, and Ember.js frameworks as well as CSS and HTML.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, Ultimate Frisbee, and rock climbing.
